Smile notes

Three things most people miss about their mouth.

A few reminders we share with patients at every check-up.

Your gums hold secrets too

Bleeding while brushing isn't "normal" — it's often the earliest sign of gum disease. Caught early, it's simple and painless to treat.

Sensitivity is treatable, not permanent

Wincing at cold water or ice cream usually points to worn enamel or exposed roots — both have quick, lasting fixes.

Pregnancy changes your dental needs

Hormonal shifts can affect gum health during pregnancy — a quick check-up each trimester protects both you and your baby.
